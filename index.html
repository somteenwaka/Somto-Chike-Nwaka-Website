About

Somto Chike-Nwaka is a lawyer with expertise in Alternative Dispute Resolution, Business Development and Operations Somto is an alumni of the Meltwater Entrepreneurial Institute of Technology. She has an LLB and a BL from the Nnamdi Azikiwe University and the Nigerian Law school.

In 2016, she launched ifenkili-by-somto, a clothing line that stocked top of the line and easy to wear african print clothing. While the clothing line has taken a back seat for the time being, Somto is still very interested and involved in the creative industry.

Somto enjoys reading, writing, listening to music, playing board games and taking long walks.
